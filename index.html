Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer massage therapy, hands-on techniques, deep tissue therapy, heat therapy, and holistic wellness treatments for pain relief and internal health support..

What conditions do you treat?

We treat a wide range of conditions including body pains, headaches, arthritis, rheumatism, stress, fatigue, cardiovascular issues, diabetes, stroke recovery, women’s health concerns, and general wellness conditions..

Is your therapy safe and How long does a therapy session last?

Yes. Our therapies are safe, non-invasive, and administered by trained professionals using natural methods. Session duration varies by treatment type, but most sessions last between 30–60 minutes.

Do you offer in-person or online therapy sessions?

Yes. We offer in-person, online, and hybrid therapy sessions depending on your needs and condition..

Do I need a doctor’s referral and How many sessions will I need??

No referral is required. You can book a session directly with us. This depends on your condition and response to treatment. A personalized therapy plan will be discussed after assessment.

Can therapy help with chronic conditions?

Yes. Our therapies are designed to support and manage chronic conditions and improve quality of life.
